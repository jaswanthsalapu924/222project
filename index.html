                    Diversification is the identity of the country. Indian dance (classical dance of India) is one of the most revered identities of our culture.
                                   Indian classical dance is an art that is in tradition for a long. The origin of Indian classical dance traces back to 
                                           “Natya Shastra” .It is a Sanskrit text. History says that the first text of this shastra dates back 
                                                   between 200 BCE and 200 CE. These classical dances grace a lady’s feminity and her
                                                       attire…Most of these dance forms are a means of communicating mythological
                                               stories and village folklore. Almost all of them have nritya and a combination of dance forms.

                                       The famous Indian classical dance forms are Bharatnatyam, kathak, kathakali, Manipuri, Kuchipudi, and Odissi.
